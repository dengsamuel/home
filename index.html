Condiments

So what are condiments? Condiments are things like Ketchup, Sriracha Chilli Sauce, and Mayonnaise. Think of condiments like the sidekicks you see in books, movies or TV shows. Examples of sidekicks would be Doctor Watson to Sherlock Holmes, Chewbacca to Han Solo, or Patrick Star to Spongebob Squarepants. Without the sidekicks, these heroes would be nowhere close to who we know them as. That's why condiments are so important. They're essentially the “sidekicks” to your food, and that's why they are essential to any meal!
